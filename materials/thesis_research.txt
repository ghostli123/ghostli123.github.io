In an era of ubiquitous connectivity, various new applications, new protocols, and online services (e.g., cloud services, distributed machine learning, cryptocurrency) have been constantly creating. Demands for securing and accelerating networks - whether backbone networks of Internet service providers, campus/enterprise networks, data center networks, or even satellite networks - have been growing rapidly. Yang's thesis is centered on designing and developing new and effective virtualized network functions and systems for intelligent network processing, with the goal of enhancing security and performance of networks.
